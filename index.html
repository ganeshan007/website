Ganeshan Malhotra

I am Ganeshan Malhotra, a junior undergraduate at BITS Pilani, Goa. My research interests span a wide range of topics in Deep Learning with a focus on Natural Language Processing and Computational Linguistics. Previously I have worked with Sumeet Saurav at CSIR-CEERI, Pilani on Driver Drowsiness Detection. I have worked on a whole range of projects ranging from Multi-modal Sentiment Analysis to designing a fully controlled elevator system.

I have also worked with Dr. Naveen Gupta to design OFDMA-NOMA techniques that could improve 5g communications. Our work was accepted at International Conference on Telecommunications, 2020.

Currently I am working with Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty and Dr. Md. Shad Akhtar at Laboratory for Computational Social Systems (LCS2) to design a multi-lingual Virtual Counselor. My research objective is to build human-like interactive agents that can comprehensively understand the world around us.
